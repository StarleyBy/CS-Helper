Clinical Notes

Indications covered: VTE treatment, ACS (NSTEMI/STEMI bridging), post-cardiac surgery anticoagulation, DVT/PE.
Renal adjustment: UFH does not require dose adjustment for renal failure; preferred over LMWH in severe CKD.
Reversal: Protamine sulfate — 1 mg per 100 U heparin given in last 2–3 hours (max 50 mg IV slow).
Hold for procedures: Stop infusion 4–6h before; check aPTT before resumption.
HIT suspicion: Stop all heparin immediately; switch to argatroban or fondaparinux.
