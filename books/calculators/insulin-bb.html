Clinical Notes — Cardiac Surgery & CICU

Basal insulin (Glargine, Detemir, Degludec) — inject once daily, same time.
Rapid-acting bolus (Aspart, Lispro) — inject immediately before each meal.
Perioperative (EACTS 2024): Start with 50% of calculated TDD after hemodynamic stability.
Transition from IV insulin: calculate 24h IV requirement → give 50% as basal insulin once oral intake resumes.
NPO patients: Give 50–75% of basal dose only. Hold bolus. Monitor glucose q4h.
Steroids: May require 30–50% TDD increase; consider afternoon/evening basal shift.
CRRT (Citrate): Insulin requirements may decrease; use conservative factor (0.3 U/kg max) and monitor glucose q2h.
