Clinical Notes

Basal insulin (e.g. Glargine, Detemir) — inject once daily, same time each day.
Rapid-acting bolus (e.g. Aspart, Lispro) — inject immediately before each meal.
Dose adjustment: Increase or decrease total dose by 10–20% based on glucose trends. Titrate every 1–2 days.
NPO patients: Give 50–75% of basal dose only. Hold bolus. Continue glucose monitoring q4h.
Steroids: May require 30–50% TDD increase; consider afternoon/evening basal shift.
