# Platelet inhibitors to be stopped prior to surgery		

| Drug (Brand name) | Mechanism of Action | Duration of effect | Discontinue before surgery | Dosing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aspirin** | Inhibits cyclooxygenase (COX) | 7 days | 3–5 days | 81–100 mg once daily |
| **Clopidogrel** (Plavix) | Inhibits ADP receptor P2Y12 (irreversible) | 7 days | 5 days | 75 mg once daily; loading 300–600 mg once |
| **Prasugrel** (Effient) | Inhibits ADP receptor P2Y12 (irreversible) | 7 days | 7 days | 5–10 mg once daily; loading 60 mg once |
| **Ticagrelor** (Brilinta) | Inhibits ADP receptor P2Y12 (reversible) | 1–2 days | 5 days | 90 mg twice daily; loading 180 mg once |
| **Cangrelor** (IV) | Inhibits ADP receptor P2Y12 (reversible) | 1–2 hours | — | Bolus 30 mcg/kg before PCI, then 4 mcg/kg/min |
| **Tirofiban** (Aggrastat) | Inhibits GP IIb/IIIa receptor | 4–6 hours | 4 hours | Bolus 25 mcg/kg IV over 5 min, then 0.15 mcg/kg/min for 18 h |
| **Eptifibatide** (Integrilin) | Inhibits GP IIb/IIIa receptor | 4–6 hours | 4 hours | Bolus 180 mcg/kg IV over 2 min, then 2 mcg/kg/min for 72–96 h |
| **Abciximab** (ReoPro) | Inhibits GP IIb/IIIa receptor | > 24 hours | 24 hours | Bolus 0.25 mcg/kg IV over 1 min, then 0.125 mcg/kg/min for 12 h |
