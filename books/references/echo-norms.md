# Echocardiography Normal Values

## Left Ventricle

| Parameter | Normal Range | Units |
|---|---|---|
| LVEDD | 42 – 58 | mm |
| LVESD | 25 – 40 | mm |
| IVS thickness (diastole) | 6 – 11 | mm |
| LVPW thickness (diastole) | 6 – 11 | mm |
| EF (Biplane Simpson) | ≥ 55 | % |
| FS (Fractional Shortening) | 25 – 45 | % |
| LV Mass Index (♂) | < 115 | g/m² |
| LV Mass Index (♀) | < 95 | g/m² |

> **Hypertrophy**: concentric if relative wall thickness > 0.42; eccentric if RWT ≤ 0.42

## Right Ventricle

| Parameter | Normal | Units |
|---|---|---|
| RV basal diameter | < 41 | mm |
| RV mid diameter | < 35 | mm |
| RV longitudinal | < 83 | mm |
| TAPSE | ≥ 17 | mm |
| S' (tissue Doppler, lateral TV) | ≥ 9.5 | cm/s |
| FAC (Fractional Area Change) | ≥ 35 | % |
| RVSP (estimated) | < 35 | mmHg |

## Left Atrium

| Parameter | Normal | Units |
|---|---|---|
| LA diameter (parasternal) | < 40 | mm |
| LA Volume Index | < 34 | mL/m² |

## Aorta

| Parameter | Normal | Units |
|---|---|---|
| Aortic annulus | 20 – 27 | mm |

| Sinotubular junction | 22 – 36 | mm |
| Ascending aorta | < 38 | mm |
| Aortic arch | < 30 | mm |
| Descending thoracic aorta | < 28 | mm |

## Diastolic Function Grading

| Grade | E/A | E/e' | LAVI | TR velocity |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Normal | 0.8–2.0 | < 14 | < 34 mL/m² | < 2.8 m/s |
| Grade I (impaired relaxation) | < 0.8 | — | — | — |
| Grade II (pseudonormal) | 0.8–2.0 | ≥ 14 | ≥ 34 | ≥ 2.8 |
| Grade III (restrictive) | > 2.0 | ≥ 14 | ≥ 34 | ≥ 2.8 |

## Pericardium
- Normal thickness: < 2 mm
- Effusion: trivial < 5 mm; small 5–10 mm; moderate 10–20 mm; large > 20 mm
