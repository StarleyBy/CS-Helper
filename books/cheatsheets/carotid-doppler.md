# Carotid & Vertebral Doppler — Interpretation

## Normal Values

| Vessel | PSV (cm/s) | EDV (cm/s) | RI |
|---|---|---|---|
| CCA (common carotid) | 60–100 | 20–40 | 0.6–0.8 |
| ICA (internal carotid) | 40–100 | 20–40 | 0.55–0.75 |
| ECA (external carotid) | 40–80 | < 15 | 0.75–0.85 |
| Vertebral artery | 30–80 | 10–30 | 0.6–0.8 |

**ICA/CCA ratio:** normal < 2.0

## ICA Stenosis Grading (NASCET criteria)

| Degree | PSV ICA | EDV ICA | ICA/CCA ratio | Plaque |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Normal | < 125 cm/s | < 40 | < 2.0 | No or < 50% |
| < 50% | < 125 | < 40 | < 2.0 | Visible, < 50% |
| 50–69% | 125–230 | 40–100 | 2.0–4.0 | ≥ 50% |
| **≥ 70%** | **> 230** | **> 100** | **> 4.0** | ≥ 50% |
| Near occlusion | Variable, low | Variable | Variable | Visible high-grade |
| Total occlusion | No flow | — | — | — |

> **Symptomatic ≥ 70%:** CEA (carotid endarterectomy) or CAS (stenting) indicated within 2 weeks

## Plaque Characterization

| Feature | Significance |
|---|---|
| Echolucent (soft/lipid) | Higher embologenic risk |
| Echogenic (calcified) | More stable; acoustic shadow |
| Heterogeneous | Increased risk |
| Ulceration | High embolic risk |
| Surface irregularity | Increased risk |

## Vertebral Artery

| Finding | Significance |
|---|---|
| Normal antegrade flow | Normal |
| Reduced PSV | Proximal stenosis or hypoplasia |
| Retrograde flow | Subclavian steal syndrome |
| Alternating flow | Pre-steal (hemodynamically significant) |
| No flow detected | Occlusion or hypoplasia |

**Subclavian steal:** BP difference > 20 mmHg between arms → hyperemic test → reversal/alternation of VA flow

## Waveform Patterns

| Pattern | Meaning |
|---|---|
| High resistance (ECA-like) | Distal vascular resistance ↑ (post ICA occlusion, severe stenosis) |
| Low resistance (ICA-like) | Normal low-resistance vascular bed |
| Dampened / tardus-parvus | Severe proximal stenosis upstream |
| Absent diastolic flow | Critically high resistance; possible vascular emergency |

## Reporting Template (minimum)
- ICA: PSV, EDV, plaque description, stenosis grade
- ICA/CCA ratio
- CCA: PSV, plaque
- ECA: PSV, plaque
- Vertebral: direction, PSV, symmetric/asymmetric
- Subclavian steal: yes / no / pre-steal
