Stroke risk assessment in non-valvular atrial fibrillation

Scoring Criteria

Total Score: 0

Interpretation & Recommendation

Score Annual Stroke Risk Recommendation
0 (♂) ~0% No therapy
1 (♂) / 0 (♀) ~1.3% Consider OAC
≥ 2 ~2.2–15.2% OAC recommended

Note: Female sex alone (score = 1 in ♀) does not independently indicate anticoagulation — at least one additional clinical risk factor required.

Preferred Anticoagulants

  • NOACs preferred over warfarin (unless valvular AF, mechanical valve, severe CKD)
  • Rivaroxaban 20 mg OD with evening meal
  • Apixaban 5 mg BID (2.5 mg BID if ≥ 2 of: age ≥ 80, weight ≤ 60 kg, Cr ≥ 133 µmol/L)
  • Dabigatran 150 mg BID (110 mg BID if age ≥ 80, high bleeding risk, or eGFR 30–50)
  • Warfarin target INR 2.0–3.0

Valvular AF — Always Anticoagulate: Regardless of score in rheumatic MS or mechanical heart valves.
