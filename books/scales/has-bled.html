HAS-BLED Score

Bleeding risk assessment in patients on anticoagulation for AF

Scoring Criteria

Total Score: 0

Interpretation

Score Risk Annual Bleeding Rate
0–1 Low ~1%
2 Moderate ~1.9%
≥ 3 High ~3.7–8.7%

Note: High score should prompt correction of modifiable factors, not automatic withholding of anticoagulation. Stroke prevention benefit usually outweighs bleeding risk if CHA₂DS₂-VASc ≥ 2.

Modifiable Risk Factors to Address

  • Uncontrolled hypertension: Optimize blood pressure.
  • Labile INR: Consider switching to NOAC.
  • Drugs: Stop antiplatelets/NSAIDs if possible.
  • Excessive alcohol: Counseling and reduction.
