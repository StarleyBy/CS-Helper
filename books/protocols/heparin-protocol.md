# Heparin Anticoagulation Protocol

> For interactive dose calculator see **Calculators → Heparin Infusion**

## Standard UFH Preparation

| Concentration | Preparation | Use case |
|---|---|---|
| 50 U/mL | 25,000 U in 500 mL NS | Standard infusion |
| 100 U/mL | 25,000 U in 250 mL NS | Fluid-restricted patients |
| 1 U/mL | 500 U in 500 mL NS | Arterial line flush |

## Indications (department-specific)

- VTE treatment (DVT / PE)
- Bridge anticoagulation (valve patients, LVAD)
- Post-cardiac surgery anticoagulation
- ACS (NSTEMI) — conservative management
- Renal failure (CKD ≥ stage 4) — preferred over LMWH

## Initiation — Weight-Based

| Step | Dose |
|---|---|
| Loading bolus | 80 U/kg IV (max 10,000 U) |
| Initial infusion | 18 U/kg/h |

## aPTT Monitoring & Adjustment

| aPTT (sec) | Ratio | Action | Rate change | Recheck |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| < 35 | < 1.2× | Rebolus 80 U/kg + ↑ rate | +4 U/kg/h | 6h |
| 35–45 | 1.2–1.5× | ↑ rate | +2 U/kg/h | 6h |
| 46–70 | 1.5–2.3× | ↑ rate | +1 U/kg/h | 6h |
| **71–100** | **2.3–3.3×** | **Therapeutic — no change** | — | 24h |
| 101–120 | 3.3–4.0× | ↓ rate | −1 U/kg/h | 6h |
| > 120 | > 4.0× | Hold 60 min, then ↓ rate | −3 U/kg/h | 6h |

Target aPTT: **70–100 seconds** (laboratory-specific; verify local therapeutic range)

## Monitoring Schedule

- **aPTT:** 6h after initiation → 6h after each change → q24h when stable ×2 → q24h
- **Platelet count:** Baseline → Day 3–5 → Day 7–10 (HIT surveillance)
- **Anti-Xa** (if aPTT unreliable — lupus anticoagulant, elevated factor VIII): target 0.3–0.7 U/mL

## Special Situations

### Pre-Procedure
- Stop heparin **4–6 hours** before
- Check aPTT before resumption
- Resume at maintenance rate (no bolus) unless INR < 2

### Cardiac Surgery (post-op)
- Resume UFH 4–6h post-op after surgical hemostasis confirmed
- Initial rate without bolus: 10–15 U/kg/h
- Target aPTT 50–70 sec (lower target early post-op)

### HIT (Heparin-Induced Thrombocytopenia)
- **Suspect HIT:** platelet drop > 50% from baseline; thrombosis; timing day 5–14
- Use 4Ts score
- **Stop all heparin immediately** (IV and flushes)
- Switch to **Argatroban** (hepatic metabolism; preferred in renal failure) or **Fondaparinux**
- DO NOT give warfarin until platelets > 150 × 10⁹/L

## Reversal

**Protamine sulfate:**
- 1 mg per 100 U heparin given in the **last 2–3 hours**
- Maximum single dose: 50 mg
- Give slowly IV over 10 minutes (risk of hypotension, bradycardia, anaphylaxis)
- If heparin given > 3h ago: no reversal usually needed (t½ ~60–90 min)

## Transition to Warfarin

- Start warfarin when therapeutic aPTT achieved
- Overlap heparin + warfarin for **minimum 5 days** AND until INR ≥ 2.0 for 2 consecutive days
- Then discontinue heparin
